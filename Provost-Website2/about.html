About Java Jam

Founded by Julio Perez after his graduation from XYZ University, Java Jam has grown to be a part of the college experience for all students in the area. Either as a retreat to cram for exams or an escape to enjoy a few hours of coffee and music, Java Jam is the go to option. There are a multitude of schedule events including music and poetry, with open mic night every Thursday for students to participate in. With the expanding menu, events and fun to be had, why not stop by to be apart of the experience!
