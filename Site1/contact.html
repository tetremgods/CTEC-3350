FAQ

What are your hours?

Weekdays 10 am to 12 am
Weekends 10 am to 2 am
Times for special events are TBA.

Do you guys have any non-coffee drinks?

Yes we do! We sell various teas, some of which are decaffinated as well. Just ask one of our skilled baristas.

What kind of events do you host?

Currently, the majority of our shows are either folk music or poetry. However, we are looking to expand our selection very soon.

How do I sign up for open-mic night?

We post a new sign up link every Sunday to our home page. We do have a limit on the number of performances that we can accomondate each night so it is first come, first served.
